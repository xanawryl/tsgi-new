About PT. THE SAKURA GREEN INDONESIA
Company Overview

PT. THE SAKURA GREEN INDONESIA was established in Indonesia on September 17, 2024.

We are dedicated to strategically supporting government agencies and private companies across various sectors in Indonesia with the development and implementation of optimal management practices.

With our experienced team of professional consultants, we work closely with our clients to consistently support innovative and sustainable management programs from planning, implementation, to evaluation.

Our guiding principles and objectives

Vision

Mission
